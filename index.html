Commander, the Imperium needs you!

  • In turn-based combat, command your forces to purge the traitor filth.
  • Click an allied (brown) tank and then click the action buttons to command the tank to move or shoot an enemy (red) tank.
  • Tanks can act once per turn and move/shoot in a straight line, including diagonals.
  • Czech hedgehogs block movement and shots - use them to your advantage.
  • Use the deselect button to clear your current tank and action choice.
  • To restart the game, press the button at the top right-hand corner of the page.

Turn Overview

1) Click a friendly unit
2) Pick an action from the action bar (right-hand side)
3) Click on the grid to perform said action
4) Repeat for all friendly units
5) The enemy will take its turn
Hint) Use the deselct button to change units
