Instructions:

  • Commander, the Emperor needs you! In turn based combat, command your forces to destroy all the traitor filth.
  • Click allied (brown) tanks to use the action buttons to move them or shoot the enemy (red) tanks.
  • Units can only act once per turn.
  • Units can only move and shoot in a straight line.
  • Clicking on a unit shows that unit's stat's on the left side of the grid. Use this to view both allied and enemy stats.
  • Each tank has a move range, shot range, firepower, and health.
  • Use the deslect button to deselect your current unit.
  • Double click the end turn button to end your turn prematurely.
  • Battlefield cover blocks movement and shots - use it to your advantage.
  • If you want to see these instructions after starting the game, click the instructions button in the action menu.
  • To restart the game, double click the button at top right hand corner of the page.
