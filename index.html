Instructions:

Commander, the Imperium needs you! In turn based combat, command your forces to destroy all the traitor filth.
Click allied (brown) tanks and then click the action buttons to move them or shoot the enemy (red) tanks.
Tanks can only act once per turn and can only move and shoot in a straight line.
Clicking on a tank shows its' stats. Each tank has a move range, shot range, firepower, and health.
Use the deslect button to deselect your current tank. Double click the end turn button to end your turn prematurely.
Czech hedgehogs block movement and shots - use them to your advantage.
If you want to see these instructions after starting the game, click the instructions button in the action menu.
To restart the game, double click the button at top right hand corner of the page.
Click below to start purging!

Turn Overview:

1) Click a friendly unit
2) Pick an action from the action bar (right hand side)
3) Click on the grid to perform the selected action
4) Repeat for all friendly units
5) The enemy will take its turn
Hint) Use the deslect button to change units
